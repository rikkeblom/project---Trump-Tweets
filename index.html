Trump Tweets

Your objective: keep Trump from tweeting for one minute!

Armed with a basket full of ripe tomatos you wait in the garden to prevent another mortally stupid tweet from the orange man in the white house.

But be careful: don't hit the other people in the garden or you might be kicked out before you can stop Trump from tweeting.

This game was created by Rikke Blom in Oct-Nov 2020 as part of the course 'Basic Animation' at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. The game should be playable by 20th November.
